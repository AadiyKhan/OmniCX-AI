# OmniCX AI

## AI-Powered Customer Intelligence and Marketing Automation Platform

OmniCX AI is an intelligent customer analytics platform that predicts customer purchase intent and automatically generates personalized marketing campaigns using Machine Learning and Generative AI.

The platform helps businesses identify high-value customers, understand their behavior, and take the next best marketing action.

---

## Features

### Customer Intent Prediction
- Predicts probability of customer conversion
- Classifies customers into intent categories:
  - HIGH
  - MEDIUM
  - LOW

### AI-Powered Recommendations
Generates next-best actions based on customer behavior:

- Marketing channel selection
- Personalized offers
- Best engagement time
- Recovery campaigns

### Generative AI Campaign Creation

Uses Google Gemini AI to automatically generate:

- Email subject lines
- Marketing messages
- Call-to-action text

### Interactive Dashboard

Modern React dashboard providing:

- Customer analytics
- AI predictions
- Campaign previews
- Marketing insights
